# Data Used
## Demographics
- **d1:** Child population by age group (Number & Percent) 
- **d2:** Child population by gender (Number & Percent)
- **d3:** Child population by nativity (Number & Percent)
- **d4**:Child population by race and ethnicity (Number & Percent)
    1. Hispanic or Latino -> **H or L**
    2. Non-Hispanic American Indian or Alaska Native alone -> **AI or AN**
    3. Non-Hispanic Asian alone -> **A**
    4. Non-Hispanic Black alone -> **B**
    5. Non-Hispanic Native Hawaiian and Other Pacific Islander alone -> **NH or PI**
    6. Non-Hispanic Two or More Race Groups -> **2 or more**
    7. Non-Hispanic White alone -> **W**
- **d5:** Children in immigrant families (Number & Percent)
- **d6:** Children in immigrant families in which resident parents are not U.S. citizens (Number & Percent)
- **d7:** Children in immigrant families in which resident parents have been in the country five years or less (Number & Percent)
